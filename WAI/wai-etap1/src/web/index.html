My hobby

I'm a huge fan of the Warhammer universe. It's grim, dark, violent, and saturated with satire, making it the perfect blend for an intriguing setting.

Warhammer, the place where contradictions party harder than a dwarf in an alehouse! Social injustice? It's the lifeblood of the realm! No "forces of good" here, just shades of grimdark gray.

Thanks to its philosophical depth, Warhammer has attracted a legion of modern artists. Their works don't just inspire us, they are giving a soul to our hobby.

The journey commenced four years ago, in those sweet times before quarantine. Since that moment, I've been engrossed in painting miniatures and playing board games during every available moment of free time.

You might inquire, "Do you truly believe that spending time in a basement with a few fellow enthusiasts, playing with tiny figurines, can be enjoyable?" My response: "I don't just believe it's fun; I know it is."

Yauheni Pyryeu, 023.M3
